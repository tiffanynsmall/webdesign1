The most efficient method of steeping tea is by using a tea bag. All you need to do is pour boiling water over the tea bag that holds dried tea leaves and wait a few minutes until your beverage is ready. Although efficient, this method is not the most preferred because the tea leaves are dried and, most often, preservatives are added to extend the tea’s shelf life. Tea is the most versatile beverage out there. This is probably why it’s the most widely consumed beverage in the world. It’s served hot or cold and it’s available in a plethora of flavors. Tea has medicinal properties and is said to protect against cancer and cardiovascular disease. And, its value isn’t limited to beverage form either. You can add tea to meals and even use tea as broth for soups which is pretty tasty.
